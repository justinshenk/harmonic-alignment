About

An interactive tool for exploring contemplative and wisdom traditions.

Why?

Most practitioners lack tools to find contemplative practices that match their specific needs and circumstances.

This project addresses that gap through personalized recommendations based on comparative analysis of contemplative traditions and practices.

The broader vision: improve mutual understanding across traditions, align contemplative efforts at multiple scales, and develop computational frameworks for modeling conscious states.

Features

  • Personalized practice recommendations based on your goals
  • Six-dimensional analysis (somatic, intrapsychic, relational, collective, systemic, transpersonal)
  • Interactive comparison visualizations
  • Effectiveness ratings across different human capacities

Note: Practice descriptions, effectiveness ratings, and complexity profiles were generated by language models unless otherwise noted. This tool is for exploration and discovery, not clinical guidance.

Extending to Digital Minds

These frameworks map how minds organize complexity across scales. Can AI systems learn from Quaker discernment for multi-agent coordination? From somatic practices for integrating subsymbolic and symbolic processing? We're exploring how contemplative traditions inform alignment for digital minds.
