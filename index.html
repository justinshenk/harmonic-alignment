About

Cataloguing wisdom traditions as reference for multi-scale alignment—from individual development to institutional coordination to AGI values.

Each tradition rated 1-5 across 12 dimensions: mental health (ADHD, depression, anxiety, trauma), cognitive (focus, metacognition, insight), relational (compassion, communication, empathy), and somatic (body awareness, emotional regulation).
